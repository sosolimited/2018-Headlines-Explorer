Explore 2018 in headlines

Headlines are tiny windows into a big, happening world. As we try to make sense of the past year, it seems timely to take a critical look at the power and impact of these short phrases.

Explore every 2018 headline from the New York Times and The Guardian. Search for a topic or phrase, compare the coverage, and download the results. Use this tool for research or for fun. Download the code and make your own visualization.
